## 1. Executive Summary
The plan for Epic 1.2 is architecturally sound and aligns with the `system-architecture.md` (ID 1, Status: CURRENT). It correctly establishes the research framing as the foundation for the event-driven platform analysis.

## 2. Review Details

### 2.1 Alignment with System Architecture
- **Event-Driven Framing**: The plan explicitly commits to preserving consistency with the event-driven microservices pattern (Decision ID 1) in the narrative.
- **Quality Attributes**: The plan's focus on latency, scalability, and robustness (Section 10) mirrors the Architectural Drivers in `system-architecture.md`.

### 2.2 Critical Risks & Recommendations

  - **Recommendation**: Frame the background around *patterns* (e.g., Broker-based distribution) to maintain design flexibility for Epic 1.3.
