## Manuscript-Facing Rules
- Use metadata, summaries, and aggregated percentiles only.
- Do not embed secrets, bearer-style URLs, raw audio, transcripts, or translated text.
- Keep schema-governance discussion at rationale level unless a later artifact explicitly requires implementation-level detail.
- If a new evidence source is introduced during drafting, add it to this ledger before it appears in manuscript prose.
