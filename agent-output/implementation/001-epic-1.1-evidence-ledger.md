## Active `v0.1.0` Baseline Decision
The active baseline for Epic 1.1 includes only EL-01 through EL-05. Those claims establish thesis structure, architectural framing, privacy-safe evidence rules, and the deferment mechanism itself.

|-----------------|-------------|--------|
| Latency, scalability, robustness, and comparison outcomes | Epic 1.5 / `v0.4.0` | Empirical evidence exists but is not yet promoted to integrity-verified thesis proof under the current baseline controls. |

## Evidence Hygiene Notes
- Debug traces, raw transcripts, raw audio, presigned URLs, and credentials remain excluded from shareable thesis evidence.
- Any later artifact promoted from deferred to active must cite both the evidence source and the governing proof-source path that establishes shareability and integrity.
