## Architecture and Design Constraints (Section 10 Alignment)
- Preserve event-driven framing consistency with architecture baseline (`agent-output/architecture/system-architecture.md`), especially terminology around latency, scalability, robustness, and evidence traceability.
- **Privacy-Safe Evidence Handling (ID 2 Audit)**: Enforce PII/PCI redaction and placeholder-based masking for manuscript-facing evidence according to `privacy-safe-logging.md`.
- **P99 Metadata Privacy**: Aggregate raw latency measurements into histogram/percentile buckets before manuscript or handoff inclusion to prevent fine-grained profiling leakage.
- **Schema Governance Framing**: If schema evolution is discussed in Introduction or Background, keep it at rationale level (safe event evolution and traceable evidence contracts) rather than implementation-mode configuration detail.
- Keep operational secret names, low-level token policies, and exact registry compatibility settings out of chapter prose unless they are explicitly required by an approved evidence source and recorded in the sanitization ledger.
- Avoid introducing claims in Introduction/Background that imply unsupported synchronous coupling or undocumented platform behavior.
- Ensure chapter framing remains compatible with downstream architecture (Epic 1.3), implementation (Epic 1.4), and evaluation synthesis (Epic 1.5).
